About Feedback

Feedback is information given to the learner or teacher about the learner's performance relative to learning goals or outcomes. It should aim towards improvement in students' learning.It can come from a teacher or someone taking a teaching role, or from peers.

It's no secret that feedback is an important component of effective learning. Feedback improves learner confidence, motivation to learn and ultimately, a learner's attainment. It's also what your people want - 65% of peoples say they want more feedback. Feedback comes in many shapes and forms.
